Bye Bye LinkedIn


Github has all the features of LinkedIn, without all the recruiter noise. This app provides mobile tools to let you manage your follows and followings. It was built out of a need to find and follow developers at tech conferences, without using linkedin.


You can...
  1.   Find and Follow Gh users.
  2.   List who is following you
  3.   List who you are following
  4.   Find and Follow Gh users.
